THIS IS WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE DO!

Anudip Foundation helps transform lives by providing aspirational livelihoods through digital inclusion in emerging economies. We are a nonprofit company with operations in India and USA. Anudip Foundation empowers & motivates young job aspirants from difficult backgrounds since 2007. This is what we do & we even help the youth with digital skills through our skill development program. For more information visit our Youtube channel!
